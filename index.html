KGNU Grateful Dead Posters

These commemorative posters were created for KGNUs Grateful Dead Rare Tape Specials that occur during our semi-annual membership drives. Each person who called during the Dead Special received a free poster. The first poster was created in 1988, but many of the earlier posters are currently out of print. Those that are still in print are offered here for sale for $10 each (plus shipping if you don't pick them up). All posters are printed in full color* on 11x17 cardstock. To order, please call KGNU at 303 449-4885.
